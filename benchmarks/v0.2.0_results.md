## Known Bottlenecks & Recommendations

> [!NOTE]
> These benchmarks measure raw subsystem latency. End-to-end ingest latency through the full pipeline (Valence → Storage → Consolidation) will be higher due to LLM API calls.

1. **Vector upsert variance**: Single vector upserts show high variance (4.3–8.2 ms) due to LanceDB's internal compaction. Batch upserts amortise this.

2. **SQLite WAL contention**: Under high concurrent write load, WAL journal growth may increase tail latencies. Monitor `P99` in production.

3. **Graph scaling**: PageRank on 20 nodes completes in <1 ms. For graphs exceeding ~100K nodes, consider migrating to the `MemgraphProvider` (roadmap).

4. **LLM latency dominates**: In production, the consolidation loop's total latency is dominated by LLM API round-trips (~200–2000 ms), not by parsing (~1 µs) or storage (~5 ms). Optimise batch sizes to maximise LLM utilisation.
